Faculty Skill Assessments

Welcome to the detailed page for the Assessments section! Here are some recommended skill assessment tools that can help evaluate your strengths and areas for improvement in various work-related competencies. Choose the one that suits your career goals and development needs.

Recommended Faculty Skill Assessments

  • 123test's Competency Test - Evaluate 16 common work-related competencies to identify strengths and areas for improvement.
  • CareerOneStop's Skills Matcher - Match your skills to potential career paths, sponsored by the U.S. Department of Labor.
  • National Careers Service Skills Assessment - Discover roles aligned with your skills and preferences, with guidance for professional development.
